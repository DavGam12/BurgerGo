Fire emergency protocol

Fires are a type of accident that can cause serious damage not only to property but also to customers and workers if proper safety measures are not followed.

Using the following image of the premises as a reference, we can observe an emergency exit on the opposite side of the entrance to facilitate the evacuation of all people who may be inside the establishment at the time of the fire. Additionally, fire extinguishers with CO2 are visible on the walls of the premises, represented by a circle and a line.

Thanks to these devices, which are distributed at various points, employees will be able to extinguish the flames for better control of the situation. If the fire has spread beyond the kitchen, it is imperative to leave the premises as soon as possible and call emergency services.
